| 2        | पु**णँ**          | [[उपदेशेऽजनुनासिक इत् (१.३.२)]]    |
| 3        | पुण्              | [[तस्य लोपः (१.३.९)]]              |

### धातु (The root verb)

Let us first take the verb root इण् from the list given in the [[धातुपाठ]]. 

| क्रमाङ्क | स्थिति   | सूत्र                           |
| -------- | -------- | ------------------------------- |
| 1        | **पुणँ** | [[भूवादयो धातवः (१.३.१)]]       |
| 2        | पु**णँ** | [[उपदेशेऽजनुनासिक इत् (१.३.२)]] |
| 3        | पुण्     | [[तस्य लोपः (१.३.९)]]           |

1. As soon as any verb root is selected from the [[धातुपाठ]], it gets the label [[धातु]]. Hence पुणँ gets the label धातु.
2. This entity पुणँ is still in its [[उपदेश | औपदेशिक]] (original) form. The अनुनासिक-स्वर (nasalised vowel)  of any entity in its original form gets the label [[इत्]]. Hence, the letter अँ gets the label इत्.
3. Any entity with the label [[इत्]] gets deleted. Therefore, अँ gets deleted[^5]. Only पुण् remains of the धातु.

### उपसर्ग (The prefix)

Adding an उपसर्ग is pretty straightforward from a procedural standpoint.

| क्रमाङ्क | स्थिति        | सूत्र                            |
| -------- | ------------- | -------------------------------- |
| 4        | **नि** + पुण् | [[उपसर्गाः क्रियायोगे (१.४.५९)]] |
| 5        | नि + पुण्     | [[ते प्राग्धातोः (१.४.८०)]]      |

4. Any word from the ready-made list of words [[प्रादिगण]] gets the label [[उपसर्ग]] when attached to a [[धातु]]. Since नि is from the list प्रादिगण, and इ is a धातु (from Step 1), therefore, अनु gets the label उपसर्ग.
5. Any उपसर्ग must be placed before the धातु. Hence नि is placed before पुण्.

### कृत्-प्रत्यय-विधान (The attachment of कृत् suffix)

Now comes the crucial part of attaching the प्रत्यय (suffix) to the धातु in the desired meaning. This is crucial because there **must** be a सूत्र for the exact combination of the धातु, प्रत्यय, and अर्थ (meaning). If such a सूत्र is absent, the suffix **cannot** be attached and the word cannot be formed.

| क्रमाङ्क | स्थिति            | सूत्र                              |
| -------- | ----------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| 6        | नि + पुण् + **क** | [[इगुपधज्ञाप्रीकिरः कः (३.१.१३५)]] |
| 7        | नि + पुण् + **क** | [[लशक्वतद्धिते (१.३.८)]]           |
| 8        | नि + पुण् + अ     | [[तस्य लोपः (१.३.९)]]              |
| 9        | **निपुण**         | [[कृत्तद्धितसमासाश्च (१.२.४६)]]    |
6. The [[प्रत्यय]] called क is added after any धातु that has इ,ई,उ,ऊ,ऋ or ॠ as its [[उपधा]] (penultimate letter)[^7] to convey the meaning of the doer of the action. Since पुण् is a धातु that has the letter उ as its penultimate letter, hence क can be attached to it in the meaning of the doer of the action[^8]. 
7. This entity अच् is still in its [[उपदेश | औपदेशिक]] (original) form. The last व्यञ्जन (vowel) of any entity in its original form gets the label [[इत्]]. Hence, the letter च् gets the label इत्.
8. Any entity with the label [[इत्]] gets deleted. Therefore, च् gets deleted[^5]. Only अ remains of the प्रत्यय.

After these transformations, we have नि + पुण् + अ. We simply say all of these together to get निपुण. And now, the procedure book sort of congratulates us on the word formation by saying

12. Any entity that ends in a कृत् प्रत्यय, तद्धित प्रत्यय, or an entity that is a समास is a meaningful word ([[प्रातिपदिक]]). Since the entity अन्वय ends in a कृत् प्रत्यय (in Step 6, read the footnote), therefore, निपुण is a प्रातिपदिक.

[^5]: Technically, nothing ever gets deleted, it is only ever hidden. But for current purposes deletion is also equivalent

[^7]: By the सूत्र [[इगुपधज्ञाप्रीकिरः कः (३.१.१३५)]], the suffix क is also ordained for the verb-roots ज्ञा, प्री, and कॄ.

[^8]: Due to the position of the सूत्र [[इगुपधज्ञाप्रीकिरः कः (३.१.१३५)]] in अष्टध्यायी, the suffix अच् gets the additional labels [[प्रत्यय]] and [[कृत्]]. It is this position that also dictates that अच् should be placed after the धातु.
