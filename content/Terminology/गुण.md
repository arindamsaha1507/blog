The letters अ, ए, ओ are labelled गुण as defined by [[अदेङ्गुणः (१.१.२)]].

गुण also refers to converting letters into अ, ए, or ओ.

When not explicitly specified, इक् letters are converted to गुण letters by [[इको गुणवृद्धी (१.१.३)]]. Using [[स्थानेऽन्तरतमः (१.१.५०)]]
- इ/ई becomes ए
- उ/ऊ becomes ओ
- ऋ/ॠ becomes अर्
- ऌ becomes अल्
The last two conversions are due to [[उरण् रपरः (१.१.५१)]].
